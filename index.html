About

Jose Vela is dedicated to providing excellent customer service. His philosophy is simple: clients come first. He pledges to be in constant communication and to keep you fully informed throughout the entire buying or selling process. He also believes in keeping in contact with past clients and helping them understand how the changes in the market could impact their buying or selling decisions.
He believes that if you are not left with an amazing experience, he has not done his job. His success is not measured through achievements or awards, but through the satisfaction of his clients. Jose takes great pride in the relationships he builds and will always work relentlessly on the client’s behalf to help them achieve their real estate goals.
Jose obtained his Real Estate License in May of 2014, obtained the Certified Negotiations Expert Designation in June 2014, and became a certified Pricing Strategy Advisor in 2016.
He has personally sold over $5 million in residential transactions. He has been rated as a 5 Star REALTOR® by Orlando Style Magazine in November of 2017. Jose is an active member of the Osceola County Association of Realtors and the National Association of Realtors.
Today’s buyers and sellers need a trusted resource that can guide them through the complex world of real estate. With his extensive knowledge and commitment to providing only the best and most timely information, Jose Vela is your go-to source for real estate industry insight and advice. He believes that “buying or selling a home is more than just a transaction: it is a life-changing experience.”
